Phase 1 — Foundation
April · Weeks 1–4
BASICS ▾
Math: Algebra, Quadratics, Functions
Physics: Kinematics, Newton's Laws, Energy
CS: Number Systems, Logic Gates, Programming Basics
English: Tenses, Grammar, 10 vocab/day
No past papers yet — build understanding first
Phase 2 — Drill + Past Papers
May · Weeks 5–8
DRILL ▾
Math: Trig identities, Calculus, Matrices, Stats
Physics: Electricity, Magnetism, Optics, Thermo
CS: Algorithms, Arrays, OS basics, Flowcharts
English: Sentence correction MCQs, Comprehension
Start 10–15 past paper MCQs daily
Phase 3 — Full Exam Mode
June–July · Weeks 9–12
FINAL ▾
No new topics — revision only
1 full mock test per week (timed)
Speed drills — 1 min per question max
Week 9: MUET · Week 10: DUET · Week 11-12: Mixed
Stop studying 1 day before exam. Sleep 7+ hrs.
